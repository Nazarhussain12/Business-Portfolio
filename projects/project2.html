Empowering the National Survey Authority of Oman Through Comprehensive GIS Training


  • About Project

    In a significant stride toward enhancing geospatial capabilities, KITS proudly collaborated with the National Survey Authority (NSA) of Oman to deliver an extensive Pix4D training workshop. This initiative aimed to empower the NSA team with advanced skills in aerial photography, GIS, drone operation, and more.

    Comprehensive Pix4D Training:

    The training program encompassed a deep dive into Pix4D, a leading software solution for photogrammetry and drone mapping. The training gave NSA staff the skills and knowledge they needed to properly utilize Pix4D's capabilities. From the basics of software operation to advanced techniques in data processing, the training covered the entire spectrum of Pix4D functionalities.

    Quality Assurance and Accuracy Enhancement:

    The workshop placed a strong emphasis on quality assurance within Pix4D to elevate the accuracy and reliability of geospatial outputs. Discussions covered strategies to address common challenges such as image distortion and environmental variables that can impact accuracy. The NSA team was able to produce geospatial data that met the highest standards of precision. This enhanced the credibility of their mapping projects.

    Proficiency in Drone Operation:

    A key focus of the workshop was on the practical aspects of working with drones, both fixed-wing and rotary. Participants learned to design precise flight plans, ensuring optimal coverage for data acquisition. Practical exercises included drone operation, GCPs (Ground Control Points) selection and identification and laying the foundation for accurate geospatial data collection.

    Geospatial Proficiency:

    Following the training, the NSA team showed improved skills in utilizing Pix4D for their geospatial projects. The acquired skills not only streamlined their workflow but also elevated the precision and quality of their aerial mapping and GIS endeavors.

    Data Analysis and Interpretation:

    The training program delved deep into the post-processing phase of Pix4D, emphasizing the critical aspect of data analysis and interpretation. They were guided through advanced techniques to extract useful information from the vast amount of aerial imagery and 3D models generated by Pix4D. By honing their skills in data interpretation, NSA professionals can now obtain important data to help in decision-making and improve information obtained from their aerial mapping activities.
